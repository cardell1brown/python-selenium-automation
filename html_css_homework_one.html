How To Cook Perfect Salmon

Ingredients:

  • Cooking oil of your choice
  • A piece of salmon from the supermarket
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions:

  1. Let your salmon thaw out to room temperature
  2. Add your cooking oil to coat your cooking pan
  3. Apply a tablespoon of salt to both sides of your salmon
  4. Apply a tablespoon of pepper to both sides of your salmon
  5. Turn on your stove and set the fire to low
  6. Place the salmon in the pan and place the pan on the stove
  7. Set a timer for 5 minutes
  8. Once the time finishes, turn over your salmon
  9. Set a timer for 5 minutes
  10. Once the timer finishes, turn off the stove
  11. Eat your salmon
